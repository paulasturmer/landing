What is Landing by Laura Sturmer?

I arrived in Canada three years ago without knowing anyone. Over the years, I met over 200 people for coffee chats, attended events in my field, learned to network and it led me to land a job in my field and industry. I currently work as an Associate Brand Manager to one of the largest department stores in Canada.
I translated the steps I took to land a job in my field into a practical step-by-step approach. I have been using this formula to help almost one hundred newcomers and Canadian job seekers directly or indirectly land their dream.
When I realized I am passionate about mentoring I decided to launch LANDING to help other professionals land their careers in CANADA.
